Keyboard Shortcuts

Arrow Keys — Rotate camera (Orbit mode)

WASD — Move camera (Free Fly mode)

Q / E — Move up / down (Free Fly)

Scroll Wheel — Zoom in/out

Middle Mouse Drag — Pan camera

Tab — Toggle camera mode

H — Toggle this help

Tools

Wall — Click/drag to place wall blocks

Open — Click/drag to clear walls (create open space)

Light — Click to toggle ceiling lights

Spawn — Click to set player spawn point

Chair — Click to place a chair prop

Eraser — Click to remove walls, lights, props
